# 🔌 Three-Phase Power Distribution & Motor Control Systems

Welcome to my industrial electrical engineering project portfolio. This repository documents hands-on installation, testing, and maintenance of low-voltage and 3-phase (400V) infrastructure, fully aligned with UK regulatory frameworks and modern factory automation requirements.

## 🛠️ Core Technical Competencies Demonstrated

### 1. Three-Phase (400V) Power Distribution
* **Installation Mastery:** Experience laying out industrial distribution networks, mounting three-phase distribution boards (DBs), terminating Steel Wire Armoured (SWA) cabling, and wiring multi-pole circuit breakers.
* **Testing & Commissioning:** Utilizing industrial multi-function testers to execute phase rotation testing, insulation resistance checks, and earth fault loop impedance (Zs) verification in line with BS 7671.

### 2. Variable Speed Drives (VSD / VFD) & Automation
* **Control Signal Wiring:** Professional termination of 3-phase incoming power, motor outputs, and low-voltage control loops (0-10V / 4-20mA instrumentation loops) for automated machine speed references.
* **Parameter Calibration:** Direct programming of acceleration/deceleration ramps, motor frequency thresholds, and electronic thermal overload limits via drive Human-Machine Interfaces (HMIs).

### 3. Industrial Motor Control & Submersible Pumps
* **Control Panel Design:** Wiring and diagnostic fault-finding on Star-Delta starter circuits, direct-on-line (DOL) starters, automated float switches, and heavy-duty contactors.
* **System Reliability:** Implementing robust preventive maintenance (PPM) routines on industrial pump motors to eliminate unexpected systemic downtime.

### 4. Safe Isolation & Compliance
* **LOTO Protocol:** Rigid adherence to safe isolation procedures using approved voltage indicators (AVIs) and Lock-Out/Tag-Out safety padlocks prior to executing any intrusive electrical works.
